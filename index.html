Welcome to Jay's Slamming Burgers!

Jay Gordon is the mastermind behind Jay’s slamming burgers. Jay grew up in the Columbia, South Carolina area and always had a love for a good ole cheeseburger. That’s why he wanted to open this restaurant and provide different options with many different toppings that you can pick from to make your burger experience the best it can be. - Jays Slamming Burgers is conveniently located at 800 Gervais Street, Columbia S.C. right in the heart of the Vista a place known for great food.
